I am currently a Ph.D student at the University of Hong Kong (HKU), where I am supervised by Prof. Kai Han. Before that, I spent wonderful years at Nankai University (NKU), supervised by Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng and received my M.Eng degree in Computer Science.

My research interests lie in the development of open and robust scene understanding systems, with specific focus on transfer learning, perception for autonomous driving, and open scene understanding, among other related areas.
